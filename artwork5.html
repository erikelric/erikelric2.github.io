The Scars One Cannot See

MEDIUM : PHOTOGRAPGY, YEAR : 2018

"It is through pain that we are forged or destroyed"

The concept was inspired based on the everyday hardship that some people live through. For those people, most of their hardships go unnoticed leaving them scars that no one will see but are certainly there.
