UNIT 01

MEDIUM : PHOTOSHOP, YEAR : 2019

The "JAEGER" refers to an armored agent designed for combat.

The idea of a JAEGER came up based on my own and other peoples' experiences. The JAEGERS are a metaphorical representation of a person's hardship in life and how they ultimately develop an armor to protect themselves. The intent behind creating this artwork was to show the pain some people experience behind the armor the use to protect themselves - an irony for sure.
